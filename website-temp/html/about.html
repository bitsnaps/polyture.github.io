Making Data Science Approachable

Polyture’s stated goal is to make data-science more approachable. In the current job market, there is a massive data science talent gap. Large organizations are aggressively hiring and the university system is not graduating enough trained individuals. The result is that most SMBs are left out of the equation.

If a company has someone who is proficient in Excel, they can use Polyture to leverage the power of a traditional data science stack. Alternatively, if an organization has data scientists, they can build new data pipelines with unrivaled speed. For some applications, a Polyture pipeline can be designed in minutes, compared to an entire day's worth of work with a traditional data science stack.

Polyture closes that skill gap by introducing the first code-optional data-pipeline design tool. Our application includes the traditional data stack tools of Data Warehousing, Dataflow, Data Transforms/Cleaning, AutoML and Visualizations all within a single easy to use application.
